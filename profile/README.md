# Coridor

Coridor is a software lab. We build emotional intelligence into AI agents.

## What we believe

Human-like emotional intelligence is the missing factor for the next phase of AI agent adoption. We work at the cutting edge of AI and machine learning. We also build mental models of self using rigorous cognitive science and neuroscience methods.

Ethics in AI sits at the center of everything we ship. Every model, pipeline, and product gets that lens.

## How we build

We pick the simplest approach to every problem. We rank engineering work by RQE (Reliability, Quality, Efficiency) in that order. Reliability is the cornerstone of how we write code and how we treat each other.
