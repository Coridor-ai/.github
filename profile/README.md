# Coridor

Coridors are synthetic personas for market research, grounded in human qualitative participant data.

We turn millions of behavioral records into synthetic people. They sit through interviews, fill out surveys, react to prototypes, and argue about pricing. They carry personality, memory, and affect, and they don't break character.

## What we believe

Human-like emotional intelligence is the missing piece for AI agents at scale. We work at the edge of AI and ML, with mental models of self built on rigorous cognitive science and neuroscience.

Ethics comes first. Every persona, pipeline, and product gets that lens.

## How we build

We pick the simplest path through every problem. We rank our work by RQE: Reliability, Quality, Efficiency (in that order). Reliability is the cornerstone of our code and how we treat each other.
